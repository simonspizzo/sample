🏕️ TENTED CAMP SAFARI

⏳ 10 Days / 9 Nights | 💰 $2,750 USD

📍 Destinations: Mkomazi | Lushoto | Pangani | Saadani | Mikumi | Ruaha

✨ Overview

Experience the ultimate Tanzanian adventure with our 10-day tented camp safari, taking you through six spectacular destinations. From the wilderness of Mkomazi to the beaches of Pangani and the wildlife-rich parks of Mikumi and Ruaha, this journey offers a perfect blend of nature, culture, and adventure. 🦁🌿🏖️

📅 Itinerary

🚐 Day 01: Arusha to Mkomazi

  • 🚐 Pickup from your hotel in Arusha and depart for Mkomazi National Park
  • ⛰️ En route, visit the Marangu foothills of Mount Kilimanjaro. Explore the traditional Chagga Huts & Waterfalls
  • 🏕️ Overnight: Babus Camp

🦁 Day 02: Mkomazi to Lushoto

  • 🦁 Morning game drive in Mkomazi National Park
  • 🍽️ Lunch, then depart for Lushoto
  • 🏨 Overnight: Mullers Mountain Lodge

🌿 Day 03: Forest Walk / Bike Safari

  • 🌿 After breakfast, enjoy a Forest Walk or optional Bike Safari for adventure and exploration
  • 🏨 Overnight: Mullers Mountain Lodge

🏖️ Day 04: Lushoto to Pangani

  • 🚐 After breakfast, depart to Pangani in the Tanga Region
  • 🏖️ Overnight: Emayani Beach Lodge

🦓 Day 05: Pangani to Saadani

  • 🚐 Depart for Saadani National Park after breakfast
  • 🦓 Afternoon game drive in the park
  • 🏨 Overnight: Saadani Safari Lodge

🌅 Day 06: Saadani to Mikumi

  • 🌅 Early morning game drive in Saadani
  • 🚐 Depart for Mikumi National Park
  • 🏕️ Overnight: Vuma Hill Tented Camp

🦒 Day 07: Mikumi National Park

  • 🦒 Full day game drive exploring Mikumi National Park's wildlife and landscapes
  • 🏕️ Overnight: Vuma Hill Tented Camp

🏞️ Day 08: Mikumi to Ruaha

  • 🚐 After breakfast, drive to Ruaha National Park, arriving by lunchtime
  • 🏞️ Overnight: Ruaha River Lodge

🦁 Day 09: Ruaha National Park

  • 🦁 Full day to explore Ruaha National Park's rugged wilderness and wildlife
  • 🏞️ Overnight: Ruaha River Lodge

✈️ Day 10: Ruaha to Dar es Salaam

  • 🍽️ After breakfast, drive to the airstrip for your flight back to Dar es Salaam

✅ What's Included

  • 🏕️ All tented camp and lodge accommodations
  • 🍽️ All meals during the safari
  • 🚙 All ground transportation in 4x4 safari vehicles
  • 🎟️ All park entry fees and conservation charges
  • 👨‍🏫 Professional English-speaking safari guide
  • 🦁 All game drives as mentioned in itinerary

❌ Not Included

  • ✈️ International and domestic flights
  • 🍷 Alcoholic and soft drinks
  • 💵 Tips and gratuities for guides and camp staff
  • 🛍️ Personal expenses and optional activities
  • 🏥 Travel insurance and visa fees
