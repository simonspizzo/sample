🏔️ 9 Days Shira Route – Kilimanjaro Climb

📅 Itinerary Overview

  • 🛏️ Day 01: Arrival and overnight in Moshi (Bed & Breakfast)
  • 🌲 Day 02: Drive to Londorossi Gate and trek through forest to Forest Camp
  • 🌄 Day 03: Trek across the Shira Plateau to Shira Camp
  • 🏞️ Day 04: Continue ascending through moorlands to Barranco Camp
  • 🧗 Day 05: Optional acclimatization day at Karanga Valley (Extra charge applies)
  • ⛏️ Day 06: Climb steeply to Barafu Camp
  • 🌅 Day 07: Midnight ascent to Stella Point and UHURU PEAK (5,895m), descend to Mweka Camp
  • 🌿 Day 08: Descend through forest to Mweka Gate and transfer to Moshi
  • 🏁 Day 09: Breakfast & Departure

📌 Highlights

  • Crossing the beautiful Shira Plateau 🏔️
  • Less crowded route with scenic views 🌄
  • Diverse terrain and great acclimatization opportunities 🎒

✅ Included

  • All park, camping, and rescue fees 🎫
  • Accommodation before and after climb 🏨
  • Experienced guides and porters 🧭
  • Meals and water on the mountain 🍽️💧

❌ Not Included

  • Tips and personal expenses 💵
  • Climbing equipment, sleeping bags 🎒🛌
  • Travel insurance and medical items 📄
