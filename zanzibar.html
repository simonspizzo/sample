🌊 ZANZIBAR – UNGUJA HALWA EXPERIENCE

⏳ 5 Days / 4 Nights | 🏛️ Stone Town • 🌿 Spice Fields • ⛵ Sailing • 🎭 Cultural Tour

🔖 Tour Reference: SSZT-Zanzibar

✨ Overview

Discover the enchanting island of Unguja, Zanzibar with this immersive 5-day tour that blends history, culture, nature, and relaxation. From UNESCO-listed Stone Town to the pristine beaches of Nungwi and the aromatic spice fields, this tour offers a perfect combination of exploration and leisure. 🌍🏝️

📅 Itinerary at a Glance

🌅 Day 1: Arrival in Zanzibar & Stone Town

  • 🍽️ Begin your adventure with a welcome lunch at Shanga Shangaa House in Arusha
  • ✈️ Transfer to the airport for your flight to Zanzibar
  • 👋 Upon arrival, a Sunny Safaris team member will greet you and transfer you to your hotel in historic Stone Town
  • 📋 Receive a pre-tour briefing outlining the activities planned for the coming days

🏛️ Day 2: Stone Town Tour

Explore the winding alleys and rich heritage of Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. 🏰

Highlights include:

  • ⛓️ Former slave market and chambers
  • 🏛️ House of Wonders (now the National Museum)
  • 🛒 Sultan Ali bin Hamoud's Central Market
  • 🏰 The Old Arab Fort (built in the 1700s)

🌿 Day 3: Spice Tour

Take part in our special Spice Tour, curated by Sunny Safaris. 🌶️

Learn about Zanzibar's world-famous spices, organically grown herbs, and their uses in:

  • 🍲 Traditional cooking
  • 🌱 Natural medicine
  • 💒 Local customs such as wedding dye ceremonies

⛵ Day 4: Safari Blue Adventure

Embark on a full-day sailing adventure aboard a traditional Ng'alawa dhow. 🚤

Cruise through the Menai Bay Conservation Area, stopping at:

  • 🏝️ Remote sandbanks
  • 🏖️ Uninhabited islands

Enjoy wildlife sightings, swimming, snorkeling, and a seafood beach barbecue. 🐠🏊‍♂️🍤

🎭 Day 5: Cultural Tour & Departure

Visit Nungwi Village in northern Zanzibar for a Cultural Village Tour. 🏘️

  • 👀 Witness everyday life, visit a local fish market (with potential sightings of 🐟 tuna, kingfish, dorado, and more)
  • 🎣 Observe fishermen catching marine life along the coral reef (🦑 squid, octopus, 🐡 puffer fish, rays)
  • 🏖️ Enjoy leisure time for beach relaxation, shopping, or sightseeing before departure

🌟 Included Highlights

  • 🏰 Guided Stone Town tour
  • 🌿 Customized spice field experience
  • ⛵ Full-day Safari Blue sailing tour
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Cultural immersion at Nungwi Village
  • ✈️ Return airport transfers
  • 🚗 All domestic transfers and excursions as outlined
